The No. 2 Persons

THE CHARACTERISTICS OF THE PERSON

This is the mediator and peace-lover. The number two indicates the desire for harmony. It is a gentle, considerate, and sensitive vibration. Ruled by the Moon. Keywords: diplomatic, warm, peaceful, sensitive. If expressed negatively: too dependent, manipulative, passive-aggressive. As lovers: Number Twos will bend over backwards to keep a relationship running smoothly. They offer emotional security to their lovers. The number two is associated with the Moon and, since the Moon rules Cancer in astrology, is similar to the Cancer vibration.

POSITIVE CHARACTERISTICS

Cooperative, Adaptable, Intuitive, Romantic, Diplomatic, Warm, Peaceful, Sensitive

NEGATIVE CHARACTERISTICS

Overly-Sensitive, Withdrawn, Easily-Offended, Doormat, Self-Deprecating, Dishonest, Wimpy, Resentful
