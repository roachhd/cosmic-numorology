The No. 4 Persons

THE CHARACTERISTICS OF THE PERSON

This is the worker. Practical, with a love of detail, Fours are trustworthy, hard-working, and helpful. Ruled by Uranus. Keywords: trustworthy, helpful, steady, logical, self-disciplined, problem-solving. If expressed negatively: contrary, stubborn, narrow. As lovers: Although steady and generally trustworthy, Fours can be quite emotional and frustrated if they feel caged in. They tend to need some level of confrontation in their love lives. A relationship that stagnates will bring out their contrary nature. They love to solve problems, and if allowed to "take on" and tackle predicaments, they are very loyal lovers.

POSITIVE CHARACTERISTICS

Traditional, Orderly, Helpful, Persevering, Steady, Logical, Self-Disciplined, Practical

NEGATIVE CHARACTERISTICS

Closed-Minded, Neurotic, Demanding, Boring, Compulsive, Perfectionist, Rude, Moralistic
